Our Story

Founded by Rizwan Rahim Chowdhury, SylhetBites was born out of a deep-rooted passion for preserving the authentic recipes passed down through generations. In today's fast-paced, highly commercialized world, finding pure, healthy, and unadulterated food can be a monumental challenge.

That is precisely why we created SylhetBites—a premium, boutique e-commerce platform dedicated exclusively to authentic homemade food products. We firmly believe that the best, most soul-satisfying meals are not manufactured in massive factories; they are crafted with love, patience, and care in a home kitchen.

Every single product featured on our platform is proudly homemade. From sourcing the finest raw, organic ingredients to the careful preparation of traditional Bangladeshi snacks, we ensure everything is processed hygienically. When you shop with us, you aren't just buying food; you are bringing home the pure, comforting, and nostalgic taste of "Maa'er haater ranna" (mother's cooking).
