Our Story

Welcome to SylhetBites, your trusted gateway to the rich, aromatic culinary heritage of Bangladesh. Founded by Rizwan Rahim Chowdhury, our platform was born out of a deep passion for preserving the authentic recipes that have been passed down through generations.

In today's fast-paced world, finding pure, healthy, and unadulterated food can be a challenge. That is why we created SylhetBites—a boutique e-commerce platform dedicated exclusively to homemade food products. We believe that the best meals aren't made in factories; they are crafted with love in a home kitchen.

Every single product featured on SylhetBites is proudly homemade. From raw, organic ingredients to carefully prepared traditional snacks, we ensure everything is processed hygienically and served with the utmost care across the Sylhet region. When you shop with us, you aren't just buying food; you are bringing home the pure, comforting taste of "Maa'er haater ranna" (mother's cooking).
